## 🚀 Next Steps for AdSense Approval

### Before Applying:

1. **Get Custom Domain** (Highly Recommended)
   - Purchase a .com, .net, or country-specific TLD
   - Example: `nwcalculate.com` or `newworldcalc.com`
   - Connect to Vercel (free SSL included)

2. **Add Google Analytics** (Optional but Recommended)
   - Track visitor behavior
   - Shows Google you're serious about the site

3. **Generate Traffic** (Recommended)
   - Get at least 50-100 daily visitors before applying
   - Share on New World communities (Reddit, Discord, Forums)
   - Post on social media

4. **Test All Features**
   - Ensure all calculators work perfectly
   - Check all links
   - Test on mobile devices

### Applying for AdSense:

1. Go to https://www.google.com/adsense
2. Sign in with Google account
3. Click "Get Started"
4. Enter your website URL
5. Fill in contact information
6. Submit application

### After Applying:

1. **Add AdSense Code**
   - Google will provide a verification code
   - Add to `app/layout.tsx` in the `<head>` section

2. **Wait for Review**
   - Typical review time: 1-2 weeks
   - Sometimes up to 4 weeks

3. **If Approved**
   - Create ad units
   - Place ads strategically (not too many)
   - Monitor performance

4. **If Rejected**
   - Read rejection reason carefully
   - Fix issues mentioned
   - Wait 2 weeks before reapplying
