# New World Calculator - Documentation

## Features Overview

### 1. Crafting Calculator

Calculate the total cost of crafting items and determine if it's profitable to craft and sell.

**Inputs:**
- Material name, quantity, and price per unit (multiple materials supported)
- Crafting fee (station tax)
- Selling price
- Trading tax percentage

**Outputs:**
- Total material cost
- Total crafting cost (materials + fees)
- Trading tax amount
- Net profit
- Profit margin percentage

**Use Cases:**
- Determine if crafting an item is profitable
- Compare different material sources
- Calculate break-even selling prices
- Optimize crafting strategies

### 2. Trading Calculator

Analyze buy-and-resell opportunities in the Trading Post.

**Inputs:**
- Buy price per item
- Buy tax percentage (default: 2.5%)
- Sell price per item
- Sell tax percentage (default: 5%)
- Quantity
- Listing fee per item

**Outputs:**
- Total buy cost (with tax)
- Total sell revenue (after tax and fees)
- Total profit
- Profit per item
- Return on Investment (ROI) percentage

**Use Cases:**
- Identify profitable trading opportunities
- Calculate margins for flipping items
- Compare different trading strategies
- Account for territory tax rates

### 3. Gear Score Calculator

Track and calculate your average gear score across all equipment slots.

**Inputs:**
- Gear score for each equipment slot:
  - Helmet
  - Chest
  - Gloves
  - Pants
  - Boots
  - Amulet
  - Ring
  - Earring
  - Weapon 1
  - Weapon 2

**Outputs:**
- Average gear score
- Total gear score
- Number of equipped slots
- Gear tier classification (Uncommon, Rare, Epic, Legendary)

**Use Cases:**
- Track gear progression
- Plan gear upgrades
- Identify weak equipment slots
- Meet gear score requirements for content

## Tips & Tricks

### Crafting Calculator Tips
1. Always include all material costs, even common ones
2. Check multiple Trading Posts for material prices
3. Factor in territory standing bonuses that reduce taxes
4. Consider crafting in territories with lower station fees

### Trading Calculator Tips
1. Buy tax is typically 2.5% but varies by territory
2. Sell tax is typically 5% but can be reduced with territory cards
3. Listing fees are returned if the item sells
4. Consider travel time and fast travel costs in your calculations

### Gear Score Calculator Tips
1. Focus on upgrading lowest gear score items first for maximum average increase
2. Jewelry (amulet, ring, earring) can significantly impact average
3. Weapons count separately in the average
4. Aim for 600+ average for endgame content

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Are the tax rates accurate?**
A: Default rates are standard, but actual rates vary by territory and your standing. Adjust the percentages to match your situation.

**Q: Does this account for crafting bonuses?**
A: No, this calculator shows base costs. Crafting bonuses (like reduced material usage) should be calculated manually.

**Q: Can I save my calculations?**
A: Currently, calculations are not saved. Use browser bookmarks or take screenshots to save your work.

**Q: Is this mobile-friendly?**
A: Yes! The calculator is fully responsive and works on all devices.
